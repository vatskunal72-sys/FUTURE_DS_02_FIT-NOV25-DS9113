This repository contains the source files and documentation for the Social Media Campaign Dashboard, which provides a visual summary and analysis of campaign performance across different platforms and quarters.
The dashboard is designed to track and visualize essential performance indicators to assess the success and efficiency of social media campaigns.
Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)Total Impressions and Spend:
Tracking volume and investment.Facebook Total: $650.69K$Instagram Total: $821.57K$Efficiency Metrics:CTR (%) (Click-Through Rate): $34.16$CPC (Cost Per Click): $89.46$Conversions and ROI (Return on Investment): Measuring ultimate business impact.
The dashboard is organized into several key visualization panels:Overall Performance Cards:
High-level summary of total impressions (Facebook, Instagram) and key efficiency rates (CTR, CPC).Quarterly Selection:
Allows filtering data by Qtr 1, Qtr 2, Qtr 3, and Qtr 4.Count of Benchmark Range by Facebook Total:
A bar/line chart showing the distribution of Facebook campaign totals against a defined benchmark range.
Sum of Clicks by Impressions and Engagements: A stacked bar chart visualizing the proportion of Clicks, Impressions, and Engagements, broken down by various engagement values (980, 1020, 1080, etc.).
Sum of Spend (₹) by Revenue (₹): A line chart illustrating the relationship between campaign spend and generated revenue.
Sum of Conversions by Spend (₹): A colorful donut/pie chart displaying the breakdown of total conversions by different spend levels.
Sum of ROI (%) and Sum of Conversions: A large gauge/ring chart prominently showing the total sum of conversions ($\mathbf{8478}$), with an indication of ROI distribution.
